About kuikdelivery.com

The domain kuikdelivery.com was part of a crypto miner network. It was active from 2016 - 2024.

When running the malware, it put your device in their Windows domain called kuikdelivery.com.

I bought the domian to prevent any malicious actor from buying it and re-using the existing infrastructure.

- alxhu

Help, my computer is part of this domain! / Help, my computer tries to connect to this domain!

The easiest thing would be to completely reinstall your Windows to ensure there is nothing left. Link to Microsoft Support page

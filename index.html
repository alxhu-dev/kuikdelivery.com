About kuikdelivery.com

The domain kuikdelivery.com was part of a malicious crypto virus, shared via cracked software.

It was active from 2016 - 2024

When running, it put your device in their Windows domain called kuikdelivery.com.

I bought the domian to prevent any malicious actor from buying it and re-using the existing infrastructure.
`kuikdelivery.com` and `www.kuikdelivery.com` point to this info page, while *.kuikdelivery.com (any other domain) return 0.0.0.0IPv4 and ::IPv6.

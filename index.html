About kuikdelivery.com

The domain kuikdelivery.com was part of a malicious crypto virus, shared via cracked software.

It was active from at least 2016 up to 2022.

When running, it put your device in their Windows computer domain called kuikdelivery.com.

Somehow the domain was available for purchase, so I bought it to prevent abuse for malicious activity.
